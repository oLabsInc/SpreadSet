SpreadSet v0.0.1

SpreadSet will run on SpreadOS (AR/VR & IoT Online Operating System). Every feature built into WideSpread, will be built into every SpreadSet with no limitations (users that access WideSpread on the internet via any other hardware will be limited in which features/data is accessible to them in order to prevent 3rd party sites/apps from collecting your user data).
