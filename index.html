SpreadSet Overview

The SpreadSet is a large cell phone sized curved touchscreen (it's basically a phone, only it's the reinvented WideSpread version). The rear side of the SpreadSet has 3 smaller touchscreens with 2 fisheye 180° HD cameras for 3D 360° VR image & video sharing (there are 2 pop-out cameras that face the opposite direction as the front 3D camera setup to ensure 3D 360° footage from your SpreadSet).
